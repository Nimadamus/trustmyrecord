Before you write

Many questions are already answered in the platform docs: how TrustMyRecord works, how pick grading works, the platform rules, the privacy policy, and DMCA / copyright. If your question is about a specific grade, include the pick, the game, and the final score so support can trace it against the grading engine quickly.
