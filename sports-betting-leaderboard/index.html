Why ROI beats raw win rate

A 70 percent win rate sounds elite until you learn it came at heavy favorites that barely paid. ROI and net units fold the odds and the stake into the score, so a steady winner at fair prices is ranked on real value. The price you pay is part of the result.

Leaderboard FAQ

How is the leaderboard ranked?

Bettors are ranked by verified public records, using stats like ROI, net units, and win rate from picks timestamped before each game and graded when it settled.
