Most bettors remember the wins and round the rest. You feel like you are up, but you cannot prove it, because the picks live in your head, in old screenshots, and in group chats where the losers quietly disappear.

Free pick tracking FAQ

Is pick tracking really free?

Yes. Create a free account, log your picks before each game, and build a public record with units, ROI, and win-loss calculated for you at no cost.

What does the tracker calculate?

Net units, ROI, win percentage, average odds, current and longest streaks, and breakdowns by sport and bet type from the picks you log.

Do I have to make my record public?

The record is built to be shared as a public, timestamped page, which is the point of proving results. Picks are logged before the game so the history stays honest.

How is this better than a spreadsheet?

A spreadsheet can be edited after a result is known and no one else can trust it. Tracked picks are timestamped at submission, graded automatically, and shareable as proof.
