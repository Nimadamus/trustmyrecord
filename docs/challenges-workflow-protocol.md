# TrustMyRecord Challenges Workflow Protocol

Current status: Open Challenges are bragging-rights-only.

Rules for future agents:

- Do not activate real-money entry fees, cash prizes, cashable tokens, paid-entry contests, user-to-user wagering, winner-takes-money logic, prize pools, platform rake, or redeemable site credit without legal and compliance review.
- Do not add fake users, fake challenges, fake records, fake money amounts, fake tokens, or fake stats to the live Challenges page.
- Every submitted open challenge must clearly display: `Bragging Rights Only. No cash prize. No paid entry.`
- Challenge creation and acceptance must use authenticated real user accounts and API-backed records.
- Pending picks and ungraded picks must not be used in official challenge scoring unless a future challenge type explicitly supports pending visibility and documents that rule.
- Money or token concepts may appear only as disabled copy labeled: `Money challenges and token contests are not active yet. This feature is pending legal and compliance review.`
