FAQ

Do I have to deposit on JustBet to enter?

No deposit is required to be tracked on the TrustMyRecord contest leaderboard. A qualifying JustBet account opened through the official contest signup link on this page is required.

What if I already have a JustBet account?

Existing accounts that were not registered through the official contest signup link on this page do not qualify. You'll need to register a new qualifying account through the link on this page.

How are picks graded?

Picks are graded automatically through the TrustMyRecord auto-grader using the standard unit P/L formula. Pushes count 0u.

Will my contest picks affect my regular TrustMyRecord public record?

No. Contest picks are stored separately and do not affect your regular public record, profile stats, grading history, or the standard sportsbook leaderboard.

When does the contest start?

Registration is open now. Register your JustBet account number on the registration page, then submit your picks through the normal Make Picks sportsbook in Contest Mode at /sportsbook/?contest=justbet-mlb. Track standings on the contest dashboard.
