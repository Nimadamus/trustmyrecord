TrustMyRecord is the social sports handicapping platform where every pick is timestamped, every record is transparent, and every claim is backed by data.

The Problem

Sports betting social media is full of self-proclaimed "experts" who claim unbelievable records but have zero proof. Screenshots get cropped. Losses get deleted. Records get fabricated. There is no accountability, and the people who actually know what they are doing get drowned out by the noise.

Every day, thousands of people follow handicappers who are lying about their track record. They pay for picks from people whose "85% win rate" conveniently only exists in their own bio. The entire space runs on trust, and that trust has been broken so many times that nobody believes anybody anymore.

The Solution

TrustMyRecord fixes this with one simple rule: every pick is locked in before the game starts, and it can never be changed or deleted.

When you submit a pick on TrustMyRecord, it gets timestamped and recorded permanently. When the game ends, the result is graded automatically. Your win/loss record, ROI, and performance stats are calculated in real time and displayed publicly on your profile. There is no way to hide a loss. There is no way to fake a streak. Your record is your record.

This means that when you see someone with a 62% win rate on TrustMyRecord, that number is real. It was earned pick by pick, verified against actual game results, and visible for anyone to audit.
