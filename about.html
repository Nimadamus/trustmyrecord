The Problem

Sports internet is full of people talking like legends with nothing behind it. Screenshots get cropped. Losses disappear. Bios get rewritten. Hot takes become victory laps when they hit and silent deletes when they miss.

The handicapping world is the worst version of that problem. Too many people sell confidence instead of proof. Too many records exist only in captions. TrustMyRecord exists because sports fans should not need to guess who is real.

The Founding Rule

TrustMyRecord is built on one rule: once a pick is submitted, it is locked, timestamped, and permanently attached to the account.

No edits. No deletions. No record resets. Wins count. Losses count. Pushes count. The profile becomes a public ledger instead of a marketing page.

That same philosophy powers the rest of the site: predictions, polls, trivia, challenges, creator sales, and rivalry talk should all connect back to public accountability.
