Join the MLB Season Prediction Waitlist What Is a Season Simulator?

An MLB season simulator is a way to play out a full baseball season before, or while, it happens. Instead of guessing one game at a time, you work through the whole picture: which teams win each division, which clubs grab the Wild Card spots, who represents each league in the World Series, and how many games the contenders win across a 162-game grind.

Some simulators run thousands of computer simulations to produce probabilities. The version we are building at TrustMyRecord puts you in that seat. You think through the matchups, the rosters, the schedule, and the storylines, then lock in your own season outlook, the same way a sharp analyst would map out the year in spring training.

This page is an early look at that experience. We are being upfront: there is no fully automated advanced simulation engine or live prediction competition on this page yet. What we are building is a place where your season predictions get recorded honestly and graded against real results, so the people who actually called it right can prove it.
