1. Buyer Terms

When you purchase a pick on the TrustMyRecord Pick Marketplace, you are buying access to a seller's sports analysis: one specific locked selection, including the side, line, and odds captured at the moment the seller locked it. The pick is revealed in your purchases dashboard only after your payment is confirmed by our payment processor (Stripe), and it remains viewable there permanently, including after the game is graded.

What you are NOT buying

  • A guaranteed outcome. Sports results are uncertain. No seller on this platform is permitted to promise wins, "locks," or risk-free profit, and TrustMyRecord makes no such promise on their behalf.
  • Betting placement. TrustMyRecord is not a sportsbook, does not accept or place wagers, and does not handle gambling funds.
  • The right to redistribute. Purchased picks are for your personal use. Sharing, reselling, screenshotting for distribution, or publishing purchased picks is prohibited and grounds for account termination.

Purchase mechanics

  • All payments are processed by Stripe. TrustMyRecord never sees your full card details.
  • Access is granted only after Stripe confirms payment to our server. A browser redirect alone does not grant access.
  • Prices are shown before checkout. The price you see is the total charged.
  • Picks cannot be purchased after the underlying game has started.

Past performance shown on seller profiles is a factual accounting of graded picks on this platform. It does not predict future results.

2. Seller Agreement

By listing picks for sale you agree to all of the following:

Eligibility and identity

  • You are a real person selling your own analysis, with an email-verified account in good standing, meeting the platform's minimum graded-pick threshold.
  • You have completed Stripe Connect onboarding, and you understand Stripe (not TrustMyRecord) verifies your identity and holds your payout details.
  • You will maintain accurate information with Stripe; failed verification or disabled charges/payouts automatically suspends your storefront.

The product

  • Buyers purchase individual locked picks. You cannot edit, delete, or replace a pick after it locks — the buyer receives exactly what your public ledger records.
  • Picks cannot be sold once the game has started.
  • You may not advertise guaranteed outcomes, "locks," risk-free profit, or invented records anywhere, on or off the platform, in connection with your storefront.

Money

  • TrustMyRecord collects a platform commission of 20% of the gross sale price of each completed sale. You are allocated the remaining 80% as a gross amount, delivered via Stripe to your connected account.
  • Your 80% allocation is gross, not net: Stripe payout schedules, connected-account fees where applicable, refunds, chargebacks, dispute recoveries, and your own taxes can reduce what you ultimately keep.
  • If a buyer is refunded or a chargeback/dispute is lost, you authorize TrustMyRecord to reverse the corresponding transfer from your connected account (proportional to the refunded amount, up to your full 80% allocation for that order).
  • You may not purchase your own listings or arrange purchases designed to inflate your sales count or record.

Standing

  • TrustMyRecord may suspend or remove your storefront at its sole discretion for policy violations, fraud signals, chargeback abuse, or conduct that damages buyer trust, with or without notice.
  • Suspension does not erase your public record or your obligations for already-completed sales.

3. Refund Policy

A purchased pick is delivered the moment it is revealed, and it cannot be "returned." Because of that:

  • After reveal: completed purchases are final. A pick losing is not grounds for a refund — you purchased analysis, not an outcome.
  • Automatic refund cases: if the purchased pick is voided by the grading engine (for example, a postponed game or a voided market) before it grades, or a technical failure prevented the pick from ever being revealed to you, contact support and the purchase will be refunded in full.
  • Duplicate charges: refunded in full for the duplicate(s).
  • Discretionary review: TrustMyRecord may issue full or partial refunds at its discretion where something went wrong that this policy does not anticipate, or where required by applicable law.

Refunds are returned to the original payment method through Stripe. When a refund is issued, the seller's corresponding allocation is reversed proportionally.

4. Chargebacks & Disputes

  • If you dispute a charge with your card issuer, access to the disputed purchase is suspended while the dispute is open.
  • If the dispute resolves in your favor with your bank, the order is closed and access remains revoked. If it resolves against you, access is restored.
  • Filing chargebacks on delivered picks instead of contacting support first is treated as chargeback abuse and may result in marketplace or account termination.
  • Sellers: lost disputes reverse your allocation for that order (see Seller Agreement).

Support: trustmyrecord.com/support — contact us before disputing a charge; most billing problems are fixed faster this way.

5. Prohibited Conduct

  • Guaranteed-outcome marketing: "guaranteed winner," "can't lose," "risk-free," "100% lock," or equivalent claims.
  • Buying your own listings, wash trading, or any scheme to inflate sales counts, records, or leaderboard positions.
  • Redistributing purchased picks (resale, group shares, public posting).
  • Selling picks based on non-public inside information, or any activity that violates applicable sports-integrity rules or law.
  • Circumventing the platform: soliciting marketplace buyers to pay you off-platform.
  • Harassment of buyers or sellers, or misrepresenting your identity or affiliation.

6. Responsible Gambling

TrustMyRecord is an analysis and record-verification platform, not a sportsbook. If you choose to bet, bet only what you can afford to lose. Buying picks does not change the underlying uncertainty of sports outcomes.

If gambling stops being fun: call or text 1-800-GAMBLER, or visit ncpgambling.org. Self-exclusion resources are available in most jurisdictions.

7. Platform Commission & Payout Disclosure

  • Platform commission: 20% of the gross purchase price, collected as a Stripe application fee at the time of payment.
  • Seller allocation: 80% of the gross purchase price, transferred to the seller's Stripe connected account.
  • Payment processing costs charged by Stripe are borne by TrustMyRecord out of its commission and never reduce the seller's 80% gross allocation.
  • Payout timing to sellers follows Stripe's standard payout schedule for the seller's account and country.

8. Taxes

Sellers are independent parties responsible for their own income taxes on marketplace earnings. Stripe may issue tax forms (for example, US 1099-K) where legally required, based on the information sellers provide during Stripe onboarding. TrustMyRecord does not provide tax advice; consult a tax professional about your obligations.

9. Age & Geographic Eligibility

You must be at least 21 years old (or the age of majority for sports-wagering-related content in your jurisdiction, whichever is higher) to buy or sell on the marketplace. You are responsible for ensuring that purchasing or selling sports-pick analysis is lawful where you live; the marketplace is not offered where such services are prohibited.

10. Marketplace Suspension Rights

TrustMyRecord may suspend individual accounts or listings, pause new purchases platform-wide, or discontinue the marketplace at any time for safety, legal, or operational reasons. Suspension of new sales never deletes your data: completed purchase records, revealed picks, and seller ledgers remain accessible per these terms.
