How Selling Picks on TrustMyRecord Works

Seller storefronts are built on top of your existing public record. Eligibility is based on the history you have already built on the platform: locked picks, automatic grading, and results nobody can edit after the fact. Buyers see that record before they spend anything, which is the entire point - you are selling picks from a track record people can inspect first.

Setup takes three steps. First, confirm your account is active and your email is verified. Second, connect your own Stripe account, which handles identity verification, secure payment processing, and payouts to your bank. Third, set your own prices for single picks or pass access. TrustMyRecord keeps a 20% platform commission on each sale and you keep the rest, with the full terms spelled out in the Marketplace Terms.

Not ready to sell yet? Create a free account and start locking picks so your record is ready when you are. You can browse existing sellers in the Pick Marketplace, read the guide to selling sports picks, or check the Sell Your Picks overview for the roadmap.
