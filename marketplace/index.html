Marketplace Rules

  • Sellers must clear eligibility: a real, email-verified account with a large enough graded-pick record, completed Stripe onboarding, and an accepted seller agreement.
  • Records are computed by TrustMyRecord's grading engine. Sellers cannot edit, delete, or hide picks — wins, losses, and cold streaks all stay on the ledger.
  • A purchased pick stays concealed until payment is confirmed, then it is revealed in your purchases dashboard.
  • Picks cannot be sold after the game has started, and a seller cannot change a pick after it is locked.
  • No seller may promise guaranteed winners, locks, or risk-free profit. Sports outcomes are uncertain; buy analysis, not certainty.
  • Sellers choose their own packages and prices — single picks, day passes, weekly passes, or monthly passes. A pass unlocks every pick the seller has for games starting inside your access window.
  • TrustMyRecord collects a 20% platform commission on each sale; sellers receive an 80% gross allocation through Stripe.
