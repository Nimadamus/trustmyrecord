How a TrustMyRecord contest works

Most free betting contests are pick'em widgets with no accountability. That means the standings are not a popularity vote and cannot be gamed after the fact. The entrant at the top of the board earned it pick by pick, and you can open the board and see exactly how.

Contest FAQ

Is it really free to enter?

Yes. Registration is free and there is no entry fee. Create an account, register for the contest, and submit picks in Contest Mode.

How are entries ranked?

By net units. Every pick is graded against actual game results, so the leaderboard reflects real performance.

Do contest picks affect my public profile?

No. Contest picks live on a separate contest leaderboard and never change your public ROI, units, win rate, or pick history.

When are picks revealed?

Picks are sealed until the game starts, so nobody can copy entries or submit after the result is known.

How many accounts can I enter with?

One. TrustMyRecord allows only one account per person and one account per household, with no exceptions. Duplicate or related household accounts may be removed from contests, suspended, or permanently banned, and entries, rankings, and prizes may be voided. See the full Platform Rules.
