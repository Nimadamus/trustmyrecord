Heads up, everyone. We just launched a Daily MLB Prediction Quiz and it runs every single day of the season.

How it works:
- Every morning the TMR Polls bot posts a fresh 10-question quiz built from that day's real MLB slate. Real players, real games, correct current teams, no made-up stuff.
- The questions are a mix and they change daily: how many total bases a hitter gets, strikeouts for a probable pitcher, over/under on earned runs, will a guy score a run, who wins the game, the game total, closest-guess numbers, and more.
- You earn points for every correct answer, and the scoring is not flat. Rarer correct answers pay more, and if you fade the model and nail an underdog pick you get a longshot bonus. Sweep the whole card and there's a perfect-quiz bonus on top.
- It auto-grades itself straight from the final box scores, so there's no waiting on anyone. The winner and the day's points show up in the top right, and your points land on your profile.
- There's also a persistent skill rating (Elo style) that ranks you against everyone else who plays, so being consistent over a full season actually counts for something.

Today's quiz is already live. Jump in here: https://trustmyrecord.com/polls/

Have fun, and let's see who can actually read a slate.
