Arena Live Streaming

We are building live streaming directly into TrustMyRecord Arena, so the community can watch verified matches as they happen, tied to the records and challenges that make them mean something. No embeds of other platforms: you will stream straight to us.

What is coming

OBS & Capture Card Streaming

Stream from OBS, a capture card, or your console's built-in tools using a standard RTMPS ingest URL. If you have streamed anywhere before, the setup will feel familiar.

Private Stream Keys

Approved streamers get a private, per-account stream key. Keys are revocable and never shared publicly.

Live Match Pages

Every stream gets a live match page: the video, the challenge it belongs to, both players' verified records, and the community's Arena Picks on the result.

Replay Archives

Completed streams roll into a replay archive linked from the match result, so disputed scores and highlight wins both have tape.

Approval-Based Access

Streaming will not be open signup. To keep the platform clean and legal, streamer accounts are approved individually, starting with active Arena competitors in good standing. You can apply now; approved applicants get access when streaming launches.

Streaming & Content Policy (Preliminary)

The full streaming policy will be published before launch. The ground rules already set:

Allowed: your own live gameplay of Arena-supported titles, match commentary, and community discussion around your verified record and challenges.

Not allowed: rebroadcasting copyrighted content (TV broadcasts, other people's streams, pay-per-view events), music you do not have rights to (we recommend disabling in-game licensed soundtrack audio), harassment, and any content unrelated to sports gaming. Violations end in suspension of streaming access, which is a privilege, not a right.

Copyright complaints: TrustMyRecord responds to valid takedown notices. A dedicated DMCA policy page and designated agent contact will be published with the streaming launch. Until then, copyright concerns can be reported through the support page.
