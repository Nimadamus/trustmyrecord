How free Arena Pick predictions work

Every head-to-head challenge with two players locked in is open for predictions until the match starts. Open the match page, pick who you think wins, and stake between 25 and 500 free Arena Points. Every new account gets a free Arena Points balance to start, and that is the only currency involved. If your call is right, your entry doubles in points. If it is wrong, those points are gone, but your wallet never is, because there is no money in it.

Predictions lock the moment a match goes live, so the board reflects real calls made before the result. Your prediction record and the community's picks both show on each match page.

Why Arena Points and not money

TrustMyRecord Arena is about verified competition and clean records, not gambling. Arena Points keep predictions fun and competitive while staying completely free. They have no cash value, cannot be bought or cashed out, and exist only to keep score of who calls matches best.
