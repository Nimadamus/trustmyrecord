How Arena leagues work

Anyone with a TrustMyRecord account can commission a league: pick the game, console, player cap, season start date, and schedule format, then write the house rules. Members report match results as they play; the opponent confirms each score, and disputed results go to admin review. Standings are computed only from confirmed results, ranked by wins and point differential. Your league record also feeds your public gaming profile, alongside your head-to-head challenge history.

Leagues are the season-long layer of Arena competition. If you want one-off matches instead, browse open head-to-head challenges. Confirmed league and challenge results both count toward the sports gaming rankings, and MLB The Show players can also compete on real in-game stats in the MLB The Show Stat League. Matches stream live on the Arena streaming page.
