What is the Sports Gaming Arena?

The Arena is where sports gamers settle it on the field and keep a record that actually means something. Challenge another player to a head-to-head match in MLB The Show, agree on the rules and format, play it out, and report the score. A result counts only when both players confirm it or an admin verifies it, so the leaderboard is built from real games, not bragging.

Every match can be streamed live straight to TrustMyRecord, so the community can watch the games as they happen and follow the players they care about. Wins and losses roll into public records and rankings you can actually trust.

Starting with MLB The Show

MLB The Show 26 is the launch title for the Arena: ranked head-to-head matchups, verified records, and free predictions on the results. More sports gaming titles follow, but the model stays the same everywhere, verified competition first.

Free to play, no wagering

TrustMyRecord Arena is free to play. There is no real-money wagering, no deposits, no payouts, and no betting odds anywhere in the Arena. Predictions use free Arena Points that have no cash value and cannot be purchased, sold, or withdrawn. It is all for bragging rights and a verified record. No money is wagered.
