TrustMyRecord 101

TrustMyRecord started, just as you could probably guess by the name, exclusively as a sports monitor with one job: bring some transparency to the sports handicapping industry. What we noticed, however, was that very few handicapping websites had any interest in having their picks monitored — even for free.

At the same time, we ended up drawing in tons of absolutely amazing "amateur" handicappers who were great at predicting games but just didn't have the business sense or inclination to run their own handicapping website. With the obvious talent of our handicapper pool — and the general consensus that running a successful sports handicapping service is more about marketing than how good of a handicapper you actually are — we decided to offer a platform for the average Joe to sell picks.

That leads us to where we are today: a sports social network with receipts. Built for handicappers AND fans — track every pick, sell winners, compete head-to-head, talk shit on the forums, smoke daily trivia, vote on polls, and challenge anyone in 2K, Madden, MLB The Show, or NHL. Whatever you do here, it's on the record.
