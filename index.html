HOW TRUSTMYRECORD WORKS

One simple cycle is the entire reason this site exists. Everything else — the feed, the forum, polls, trivia, gaming, selling picks — is built on top of it.

  1. 01

    Make Your Pick

    Choose the sport, the game, the market, the side, the odds, and the units. The full ticket lives on your profile from the moment you submit it.

  2. 02

    Lock It In

    Once posted, the pick is part of your public record. No edits, no deletes, no "I meant the other side" do-overs. The receipt is the receipt.

  3. 03

    Track the Result

    The auto-grader settles every pick when the game finishes. Wins, losses, units, ROI, and win % update on your profile and feed automatically.

  4. 04

    Build Credibility

    Climb the leaderboards by sport, unit profit, ROI, and win rate. A real public record is the difference between a fan with opinions and a capper worth following.
