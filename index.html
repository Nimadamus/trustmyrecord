HOW TRUSTMYRECORD WORKS

One simple cycle is the entire reason this site exists. Everything else — the feed, the forum, polls, trivia, gaming, selling picks — is built on top of it.

  1. 01

    Make Your Pick

    Choose the sport, the game, the market, the side, the odds, and the units. The full ticket lives on your profile from the moment you submit it.

  2. 02

    Lock It In

    Once posted, the pick is part of your public record. No edits, no deletes, no "I meant the other side" do-overs. The receipt is the receipt.

  3. 03

    Track the Result

    The auto-grader settles every pick when the game finishes. Wins, losses, units, ROI, and win % update on your profile and feed automatically.

  4. 04

    Build Credibility

    Climb the leaderboards by sport, unit profit, ROI, and win rate. A real public record is the difference between a fan with opinions and a capper worth following.

The 2026 Accountability Era: Data Integrity Redefined

Welcome to TrustMyRecord, the world's most rigorous sports social network. In 2026, the picks industry is saturated with false claims and deleted losses. We have built a technical fortress dedicated to one goal: absolute truth in sports betting data.

1. The Anti-Delete Pick Policy

On TrustMyRecord, once a pick is locked and the game starts, it is permanent. Our database architecture prevents any modifications or deletions of historical picks. This eliminates record scrubbing and ensures that every handicapper's 2026 ROI is an accurate reflection of their skill.

2. Timestamped Market Verification

Every pick sold or shared in our marketplace carries a cryptographic timestamp. We verify the lines against real-time 2026 market data from major books like DraftKings and FanDuel. If a handicapper claims a line that never existed, our system flags it instantly.

3. ROI vs. Hype: The Leaderboard Truth

Our leaderboards are ranked by units won and ROI, not by social media followers or marketing budget. We provide deep-dive analytics into every capper's performance, including closing line value (CLV) analysis. In 2026, we don't just track if you win, we track how you win.
