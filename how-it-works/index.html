How TrustMyRecord Works

One simple cycle: post the play, lock the record, climb the board. Every pick is timestamped, auto-graded against real results, and permanently public. No edits, no deletes, no rewritten history.

The Core Loop

01

Make Your Pick

Choose the sport, the game, the market, the side, the odds, and the units on the picks board. Moneylines, spreads, totals, and alternate lines across MLB, NBA, NFL, NHL, soccer, tennis, and more. The full ticket lives on your profile from the moment you submit it.

02

Lock It In

Once posted, the pick is part of your public record. It is timestamped before the game starts and cannot be edited or deleted. The timestamp is your receipt.

03

Auto-Grading Against Real Results

When the game finishes, the pick grades automatically against the final score. Wins, losses, and pushes settle without any manual input, so a record on TrustMyRecord cannot be massaged after the fact.

04

Your Verified Record Updates

ROI, unit profit, win rate, sample size, and streaks update on your public profile and on the leaderboards. Anyone can audit your full history, sport by sport.

How Grading Works

Every pick grades automatically against the official final score. Wins add unit profit at the locked odds, losses subtract the risked units, and pushes settle at zero. Pending picks never count toward a record until they settle, and a settled grade can never be edited or deleted.

The full methodology, including the exact unit profit formula, push and void rules, and where results come from, is documented on the how pick grading works page.

Why It Matters

The pick-selling industry has run for decades on deleted losses, invented win rates, and records nobody can check. TrustMyRecord exists to fix that: every record on the platform is locked, timestamped, auto-graded, and public. If someone claims a record here, you can open their profile and check it yourself.

TrustMyRecord is not a sportsbook and no real money is wagered on the site. It is a tracking, verification, and competition platform.
