Soccer markets you can track

Soccer betting lives or dies on the three-way result, so the tracker treats the draw as a first-class outcome rather than an afterthought. Log the match result (1X2), double chance, draw no bet, European and Asian handicaps, totals such as over/under 2.5 goals, team totals, and both teams to score where your book offers it. When you log a pick, TrustMyRecord stores the exact selection and price and stamps it before kickoff, so the number you committed to is frozen on the record forever.

Once the final whistle goes, the auto-grader reads the 90-minute result, including stoppage time, and settles 1X2, BTTS and totals against that scoreline, then updates your net units, ROI, average odds, win rate and current streak. A draw grades as a draw. A draw-no-bet refund grades as a push. Nothing is reinterpreted after you have seen the result.

Why verified soccer records are harder, and matter more

No sport is easier to cherry-pick than soccer. A tipster can post a Saturday Premier League winner, stay silent through three midweek Europa League losers, then reappear for the weekend. With a three-way market, even a coin-flipper hits the draw often enough to fill a highlight reel. Add the volume of friendlies, qualifiers, lower divisions and a World Cup every four years and a raw "we went 9-2 last week" claim becomes impossible to audit. A locked, timestamped record fixes that: the losing 1X2 tickets and the missed BTTS bets stay on the page next to the winners, graded the same way every time.

Frequently asked questions

How does the tracker grade a soccer draw?
The 1X2 result is settled on the 90-minute scoreline including stoppage time, so a draw grades as a draw on the moneyline rather than being pushed or ignored. Double chance, draw no bet and BTTS are graded against that same official result.
Can I track picks across different leagues and tournaments?
Yes. Premier League, La Liga, Champions League, Europa League, MLS, Liga MX, World Cup and Euro qualifiers, domestic cups and international friendlies all roll into one verified soccer record, and your profile shows a competition-by-competition breakdown.
Which soccer markets can I log?
Match result (1X2) including the draw, double chance, draw no bet, Asian and European handicaps, totals such as over/under 2.5 goals, team totals, and both teams to score where supported. Each locks before kickoff and auto-grades on the final result.
Why do you track units and ROI instead of just win percentage?
A long string of short-priced favorites and double-chance bets can win most of the time and still lose money. Units and ROI measure actual profitability over a real sample size.
