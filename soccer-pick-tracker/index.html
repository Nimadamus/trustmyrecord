What the Soccer pick tracker does

When you log a Soccer pick, TrustMyRecord stores the exact selection and odds and locks a timestamp before the game starts. Units, ROI, average odds, and sample size

TrustMyRecord scores Soccer performance the way sharp bettors actually think about it. Units measure profit independent of how much money you bet. ROI shows return per unit risked, which exposes whether someone profits or just wins low-value favorites. Average odds reveal the kind of prices a handicapper plays, and sample size tells you how much to trust the number. A +8 unit record over 300 picks means far more than a hot week.
