How public pick tracking works

A pick becomes part of a member's public record only through the same locked, auto-graded process used everywhere on TrustMyRecord. The timestamp proves when the pick was made and at what odds; the auto-grader settles it once results are final. Because losers are recorded exactly like winners, the resulting history is honest in a way a highlight reel never is.

Frequently asked questions

Are picks revealed before the game starts?
Public archive pages only show what is already allowed to be public at that time. Picks that are meant to stay hidden until the game starts are not exposed early. Locked, pending picks become fully visible and graded after results are final.
How are public picks graded?
Automatically. Each pick locks with a timestamp, then the auto-grader settles it against the official final result and box score, updating the member's verified record.
Is this a place to buy picks?
No. TrustMyRecord does not sell picks and is not a sportsbook. Public picks exist to make records transparent and auditable.

TrustMyRecord is not a sportsbook and does not accept wagers. We do not sell picks or guarantee betting results. TrustMyRecord is a free record-tracking, verification, leaderboard and community platform where members log picks with locked timestamps that are auto-graded against final results to build a transparent public record measured in units and ROI.
