TrustMyRecord Site Directory

This directory is the complete crawlable index of TrustMyRecord's public pages. TrustMyRecord is a free platform where sports bettors and handicappers lock picks before games start, get every pick graded automatically, and build a public record nobody can edit after the fact. Use the sections below to reach any public area of the site: pick tracking and verified records, leaderboards, statistical guides, analysis tools like the MLB Simulator and Model Builder, the Arena console-gaming hub, contests, and community features. Private account areas and pending-pick screens are intentionally excluded.
