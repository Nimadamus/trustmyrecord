Advanced Metrics
Computed deterministically from stored fields. Anything without enough sample shows "—".
MetricValueDefinition
eU (Effective Units)—(net_units / avg_units_risked) × 3 — strips max-bet juicing.
eWP (Effective Win %)—Win-rate equivalent if every pick had been -110.
Z-Score—Standardized skill-vs-luck signal at avg-implied-prob baseline. Hidden until 5+ decisions.
AOP (Avg Odds per Pick)—Average implied probability converted back to American.
AUR (Avg Units Risked)—Mean stake across settled picks.
ADP (Avg Daily Picks)—Total picks / days active. Hidden until first/last pick exist.
Push Rate—Pushes / total settled.
Consistency Score—Inverse-stddev of monthly net units. Hidden until 3+ active months.
Capper Rating—Composite (ROI · sample · eWP · Z · drawdown · consistency). Hidden until 25+ decisions.
Max Drawdown—Peak-to-trough net units along the equity curve.
Peak Units—All-time high cumulative net units.
CLV (Closing Line Value)—Unavailable until closing-line tracking is enabled on the picks table. No estimates, per the no-fake-data rule.
Note: CLV is intentionally blank. The picks schema does not yet store closing odds, so no honest CLV exists. The backend already returns clv: null; the frontend reflects that instead of inventing a value.
