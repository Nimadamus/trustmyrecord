TrustMyRecord Platform Rules

TrustMyRecord exists for one reason: records you can trust. These rules protect the integrity of every verified record, contest, leaderboard, and public betting performance stat on the platform. They apply to every account, with no exceptions.

Rule 1: Account Integrity Rule

Most Important Rule

1One Account Per Person. One Account Per Household.

TrustMyRecord allows only one account per person and one account per household. No exceptions.

Users may not create multiple accounts, allow relatives, roommates, household members, friends, or anyone else in the same household to create additional accounts for the purpose of making picks, entering contests, improving standings, or manipulating records.

This rule exists to protect the integrity of verified records, contests, leaderboards, and public betting performance data.

Duplicate accounts will be deleted. Any duplicate, suspicious, or related household accounts may be reviewed and, if confirmed, deleted, removed from contests, permanently banned, and voided from leaderboards, prizes, verified records, and contest results.

Contest entries, pick records, leaderboard rankings, prizes, and verified records may be voided if a user is found to be violating this rule.

Rule 2: Pick Integrity

2Locked Picks, Real Results, No Rewrites

  • Every pick must be submitted before the relevant game or event begins.
  • Once submitted, picks are locked and timestamped. They cannot be edited, deleted, or backdated.
  • All picks grade automatically against verified final results. See how graded picks and pending picks work.
  • Attempting to manipulate a record through any means, including exploiting bugs or submitting picks after an event starts, is grounds for immediate account termination.

Rule 3: Contest Fairness

3One Entry, Sealed Picks, Equal Footing

  • One contest entry per person and per household, consistent with the Account Integrity Rule above.
  • Contest picks are sealed until the game starts so entries cannot be copied or submitted in hindsight.
  • Coordinating picks across accounts, sharing entries, or any other form of collusion is prohibited.
  • Violations may result in disqualification, voided entries, forfeited prizes, and account bans.

See the active contests page for contest-specific rules.

Rule 4: Community Conduct

4Real People, Real Respect

  • No harassment, bullying, or hate speech anywhere on the platform.
  • No spam, unsolicited promotion, or unauthorized advertising.
  • No impersonation of other users, public figures, or entities.
  • No illegal content of any kind.

Rule 5: Eligibility and Platform Scope

518+, No Real-Money Wagering

  • You must be at least 18 years of age to use TrustMyRecord.
  • TrustMyRecord is not a sportsbook and no real money is wagered on the platform. It is a record tracking, verification, and competition platform.
  • Account information you provide must be accurate and complete.

Enforcement

How These Rules Are Enforced

TrustMyRecord actively monitors for duplicate accounts, related household accounts, coordinated picking patterns, and other integrity risks. Flagged accounts are reviewed by the TrustMyRecord team before action is taken. Flags alone never trigger automatic penalties, because shared networks can produce false positives.

Once a duplicate account is confirmed, it will be deleted. Confirmed violations are also removed from contests, permanently banned, and voided from leaderboards, prizes, verified records, and contest results. Depending on severity, enforcement may additionally include warnings or temporary suspension. All enforcement decisions are made at the sole discretion of TrustMyRecord.

These rules work together with the full Terms of Service. If you believe an account has been flagged in error, contact support@trustmyrecord.com.
