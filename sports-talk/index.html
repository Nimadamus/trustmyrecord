About Sports Talk

Sports Talk is the fandom side of TrustMyRecord: teams, games, news, debates, fantasy, and the hobby. Verified picks, records, and handicapping stay in the Betting Forum. One account works everywhere — follow people you like and reply from any device.

Talk sports with people who put their name on it

Sports Talk is TrustMyRecord's general sports community: NFL, NBA, MLB, NHL, college football and basketball, soccer, combat sports, golf, motorsports, fantasy sports, sports gaming, and sports collectibles. Start a thread about a trade, argue a ranking, run through your fantasy roster, or show off a card. You never need to make a pick to join the conversation, and when you do want to track calls publicly, your same account carries a verified record on the handicapping side of the site.
