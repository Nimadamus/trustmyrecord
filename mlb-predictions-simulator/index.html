What is an MLB predictions simulator?

An MLB predictions simulator is a tool for testing a pick before you commit it. You simulate the matchup, see a projected winner and win probability, and use that to inform your decision. On TrustMyRecord the simulator is the research step and your locked, auto-graded picks are the proof.

How is this different from an MLB simulator?

It is the same engine used with a betting mindset. The MLB Simulator runs the matchup; calling it a predictions simulator just emphasizes the goal, turning a simulation into a confident, testable prediction you can then put on your record.

Can I make money using a predictions simulator?

No tool guarantees profit. A simulator can help you find spots where your read disagrees with the market, which is where edges live, but discipline, bankroll management, and honest record-keeping decide long-term results. That is why tracking real outcomes matters more than any single projection.

How do I prove my predictions are good?

Lock them on TrustMyRecord. Picks are timestamped before game time, auto-graded, and counted on a public profile with units, ROI, win percentage, CLV, and streaks. Over time that record, not a screenshot, shows whether your predictions actually win.
