Why screenshots and spreadsheets are not enough

A screenshot only proves one thing happened once. It says nothing about the bets cropped out of frame, and it can be staged or posted after the result is already known. As proof of a record, it is worthless.

A private spreadsheet is more honest with yourself, but no one else can trust it, because you can edit any cell after the fact. Stop guessing your record. The only version anyone can rely on is one that was locked in before the game and cannot be changed afterward.
