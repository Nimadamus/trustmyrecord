Track wins and losses you can prove

Most bettors track their record in their head or a private spreadsheet, which is exactly why nobody believes anyone online. Frequently asked questions

What is a win/loss tracker?

A win/loss tracker records the outcome of every bet so you can see your true W-L-P, net units, and ROI over time instead of relying on memory or selective screenshots.

Is TrustMyRecord a free win/loss tracker?

Yes. Creating an account and tracking your wins and losses is free. Picks lock before game time and grade automatically from final scores.

Why not just use a spreadsheet?

A spreadsheet can be edited after the fact, so it proves nothing to anyone else. TrustMyRecord locks each pick with its odds and timestamp and blocks edits and deletions, so your win/loss record is verifiable.

Does the tracker count pending bets?

No. Pending picks stay public and timestamped but are excluded from your win/loss totals, units, and ROI until the autograder settles them.
