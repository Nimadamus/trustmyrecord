What is an MLB playoff simulator?

An MLB playoff simulator is a tool for projecting the postseason: you set the Wild Card field, advance teams through the Division and League Championship Series, and crown a World Series champion. On TrustMyRecord you can lock that bracket so it is timestamped and graded against the real playoffs.

How do I simulate the MLB playoff bracket?

Use the MLB Season Simulator to set your division winners and wild cards, which seeds a full October bracket, then choose each pennant winner and your World Series champion. To simulate a single postseason matchup with win probabilities, use the MLB Simulator game tool.

Can a playoff simulator predict the World Series winner?

It can project one. A simulator estimates likely outcomes based on team strength, pitching, and matchups, but October baseball is volatile and upsets are common. Treat the projected champion as a strong opinion to test, not a guarantee.

Is the MLB playoff simulator free?

Yes. Building and locking your bracket is completely free with a TrustMyRecord account, and your locked postseason calls go on your public, graded record.
