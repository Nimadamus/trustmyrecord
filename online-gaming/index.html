How online sports gaming challenges work

Create an open challenge for the game you want, choose your platform and your GamerTag or PSN ID, and add optional details or trash talk. Another member accepts, you play, and the result is logged. Wins and losses update your dedicated Online Gaming record on your profile, completely separate from your sports betting and handicapping record. Want to challenge specific rivals on picks instead of games? Visit Challenges or the Arena. Ready to start? Browse the open challenges above or post your own.

Bragging rights and reputation only. TrustMyRecord does not process cash prizes, paid entry, or wagering for online gaming challenges.
